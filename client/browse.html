Welcome

1. Enter an artist or album name.

In the artist and album name field on the form above, enter the name of an artist or album title you would like to search for.

Underneath the title field is a dropdown for you to select your music format.

Click 'search' and then either scroll to the bottom of the page or click 'browse' in the page navigation bar to see your results.

2. Select records to add to your collection.

Click the three lines icon to view more information about a specific result, or click the plus icon to add the result to your collection.

When you're ready to view you collection, click 'show collection' underneath the displayed results.

3. View or manage your collection.

Click 'manage collection' to view and manage your saved items. You can also export your collection to a PDF or CSV.
